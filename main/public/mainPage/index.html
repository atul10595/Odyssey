Who are we?

Aligned with a pursuit of innovation and with "Alternate Reality" as our theme for this year, we present to you, IIIT-Delhi's second Annual Cultural Festival, Odyssey'15!
The best of the artists from all over India will be invited to the two day long fest. It will witness some brilliant talent perform , compete, collaborate and excel in various fields and competitions. We call you to this grand occasion to join hands and support us in our endeavour.
